About Lucoze

Making world-class healthcare management accessible to every facility.

Our Story

Lucoze was founded by healthcare professionals and technologists who witnessed first-hand the painful gap between expensive, bloated enterprise systems and the inadequate spreadsheets that most clinics were forced to rely on. Doctors were spending more time wrestling with software than caring for patients. Lab technicians were printing results and manually filing them. Pharmacists were tracking inventory on paper.

We set out to change that. Our mission is to democratize healthcare management technology — to give every clinic, hospital, diagnostic centre, and pharmacy access to the same powerful tools that were once reserved for large hospital chains with million-dollar IT budgets.

Our Mission

We believe that every healthcare facility, regardless of size or location, deserves software that truly understands their workflows. From solo practitioners running a single consultation room to sprawling hospital networks with hundreds of departments, Lucoze adapts to the way healthcare is actually delivered — across every country, every specialty, and every scale. Our goal is simple: let healthcare professionals focus on patients, not paperwork.

Healthcare First
