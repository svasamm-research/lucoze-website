Clinic & Polyclinic Management

Everything a modern clinic needs — from solo practices to multi-doctor polyclinics.

Running a clinic shouldn't mean drowning in paperwork. Lucoze brings together patient management, appointment scheduling, clinical encounters, prescriptions, and billing into a single, intuitive workflow that lets you focus on what matters most — your patients.

Smart Scheduling

Online appointment booking with practitioner availability, slot management, and automated reminders. Reduce no-shows by up to 40%.

Digital Patient Records

Complete patient history at your fingertips — demographics, vitals, encounters, prescriptions, lab results, and documents in one view.

Clinical Encounters

Structured encounter forms with diagnosis (ICD-10), symptoms, prescriptions, and clinical notes. Templates for faster documentation.

Key Features for Clinics

Prescription Management

Generate prescriptions with drug database integration, dosage suggestions, and print-ready formats. Track medication history across visits.

Billing & Invoicing

Create invoices for consultations, procedures, and services. Support for GST (India), VAT (Middle East), and international tax formats. Accept online payments.

Basic Lab Integration

Order lab tests, receive results, and attach them to patient records. Upgrade to Clinic Plus for full in-house lab management.

Multi-Doctor Support

Manage multiple practitioners with individual schedules, consultation fees, and department assignments. Each doctor sees only their patients.

Reports & Analytics

Track patient footfall, revenue, consultation patterns, and outstanding payments with built-in dashboards and exportable reports.

ABDM / ABHA Ready

For Indian clinics — integrate with Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission, verify ABHA IDs, and share health records on the national network.
