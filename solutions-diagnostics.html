Diagnostic Centre & Lab Management

From sample collection to report delivery — streamline your entire lab workflow.

Diagnostic centres and pathology labs handle thousands of samples daily. Lucoze eliminates manual tracking with barcode-based sample management, structured result entry templates, automated report generation, and quality control workflows — whether you run a single lab or a network of collection centres.

Sample Management

Barcode generation, sample collection tracking, tube labelling, and chain-of-custody from collection point to analyser.

Result Templates

Pre-configured templates for CBC, LFT, RFT, thyroid panels, and hundreds more. Normal ranges, units, and flags built in.

Quality Control

Internal QC tracking, Levey-Jennings charts, Westgard rules, and external quality assessment (EQA) management.

Key Features for Diagnostic Centres

Test Catalogue Management

Configure test panels, individual tests, profiles, and packages. Set pricing per test, department-wise routing, and turnaround time targets.

Multi-Branch Support

Manage collection centres across the city with centralised processing. Track samples across locations with real-time status updates.

Automated Report Generation

Results entered once, formatted into professional PDF reports with your branding, doctor sign-off, and critical value highlighting.

Patient Portal

Patients can view and download their reports online. Reduce front-desk queues and phone calls for report collection.

Referring Doctor Management

Track referrals, share results with referring doctors, and manage commission or referral fee structures.

Billing & Accounting

Itemised billing, credit accounts for corporates and hospitals, outstanding tracking, and GST/VAT compliant invoicing.
