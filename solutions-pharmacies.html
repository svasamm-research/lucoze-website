Pharmacy Management

Complete pharmacy operations — from dispensing to inventory to multi-store management.

Modern pharmacies need more than a billing counter. They need real-time stock visibility, automated reorder alerts, expiry tracking, supplier management, and GST-compliant invoicing. Lucoze gives independent pharmacies and chains the tools to run efficiently and profitably.

Inventory Control

Real-time stock levels across stores. Batch tracking, expiry alerts 90/60/30 days out, and automated reorder points based on sales velocity.

Fast Billing

Scan barcodes, auto-populate drug details, apply discounts, and generate GST/VAT invoices in seconds. Supports cash, card, and UPI payments.

Expiry Management

Never sell expired stock again. Automatic alerts, near-expiry reports, and return-to-vendor workflows for expiring inventory.

Key Features for Pharmacies

Drug Database

Pre-loaded with thousands of medicines (generic and brand names), formulations, schedules, and substitutes. Search by name, salt, or barcode.

Purchase & Supplier Management

Create purchase orders, track deliveries, manage supplier pricing and credit terms, and reconcile purchase invoices automatically.

Multi-Store Operations

Central procurement, inter-store transfers, store-wise P&L, and consolidated reporting for pharmacy chains with up to 10 locations.

Returns Management

Handle customer returns and vendor returns with full audit trails. Credit notes and replacement workflows built in.

GST & Tax Compliance

Automatic GST calculation by drug schedule (5%, 12%, 18%), GSTR filing support, and HSN code mapping. International VAT supported.

Sales Analytics

Track bestsellers, slow movers, profit margins by product, daily/weekly/monthly sales trends, and customer purchase history.
